Our Journey & History

1983

Establishment

Sri Sugatha Dhamma School was founded in 1983 under the guidance of Ven. suramya kiththi thero. It started with humble beginnings with just 18 students and 03 teachers.

2005

Infrastructure Development

With the support of the community, new buildings were constructed to accommodate the growing number of students, enhancing the learning environment.

2025 (Today)

Current Status

Today, we educate over 450 students, standing tall as a beacon of Dhamma education in Buttala, blending traditional values with modern teaching methods.
